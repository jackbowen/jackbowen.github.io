Eurytopic Pursuits

I was curious to see if my productivity was affected by a variety of spatiotemporal conditions so I built a rough device to record and log the factors that I believed to be of interest. There were specific sensors for objective measurements, such as the brightness and volume of my surroundings, as well as dials for more subjective metrics such as ambiance or personal space.
